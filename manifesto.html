Manifesto

We created the Subutai Platform to leverage the power of Open Source innovation with Peer-to-Peer (P2P) networks, Cloud Computing, and Blockchain technologies to bring secure, optimized Multi-access Edge Cloud (MEC) applications. Subutai empowers users to rise above the limitations of Big Cloud providers and create decentralized Edge data centers at reduced costs. Become a part of the Subutai Horde: join us in the Subutai Bazaar and start your campaign for Cloud independence, security, and privacy.

History shows us that great power can be unleashed by meritocracy, equality, independence, sharing, and collective action. Genghis Khan’s vision to depart from Mongol tradition and make Subutai, a non-Mongol, the general of his armies gave birth to the largest empire in all of human history. We chose Subutai as our project’s namesake. We believe his life and achievements epitomize the results of our common principles: meritocracy, equality, independence, sharing, and collective action.

Users at all technical levels --from novices to businesses to Cloud experts-- need to be able to easily and affordably stand up Cloud and IoT applications as effortlessly as they install apps on their smartphones.

Subutai enables and rewards both users and application developers by ensuring a rich ecosystem of Open Source applications can flourish. Application developers can package their containerized apps for users to access from the Subutai Bazaar and run on their P2P Clouds.

We want clouds to be more efficient by running closer to user load: on the Edge. By enabling everyone to monetize the value of idle compute resources, run Edge Cloud applications, as well as consume and provide cloud services in a safe and productive environment, a cloud peer sharing economy emerges with disruptive effects that benefit the collective community by accelerating the race to zero.

Open Source software and hardware are the backbone of our organization. We encourage the development of more and better products that benefit home users and businesses of all sizes. Our Open Source contribution program allows people to contribute resources towards GitHub projects as well as Subutai blueprints and applications available to the public on the Subutai Bazaar.

We are committed to supporting the environment by reducing electricity draw and senseless e-waste with throwaway products and bricked devices that are choking landfills.

We want the Internet to be a better place for personal and business computing.

We created Subutai for you to conquer the Cloud!
