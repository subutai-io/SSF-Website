KHAN™

The staking token of the Subutai platform

• Simple Ethereum Blockchain token (fixed money supply)
• Default and ubiquitous across Subutai Bazaars
• Utility token to enable commercial Open Source and more
• Platform reserve currency across ISPs like the US Dollar
• For provider and consumer reputation “staking” via service level agreement (SLA) escrows
• Escrowed KHAN interest rate earns GoodWill
• Direct exchange with automated SIPS market maker for GoodWill
• Exchange rates protect the KHAN from inflationary forces in GoodWill monetary policies (incentivization) needed to stimulate market growth
• The KHAN protects providers and consumers
