KHAN™

The token of the Subutai platform.



  • An Ethereum Blockchain-based platform token.
  • The default and ubiquitous currency across the Subutai Platform (one of two tokens backing GoodWill).
  • Automated exchange with other currencies.
  • Used by all bazaars, both those operated by economy operators and by OptDyn.
  • Only KHAN can be used for Service Level Agreements (SLA) based on Smart Contracts with escrow accounts.
  • Will have even wider commercial usage in the future, beyond purchasing resources.
  • Serves as the reserve currency of the platform across ISPs, much like the US dollar does across countries.
